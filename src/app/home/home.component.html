THE BAND

We love Movie

Welcome to the world of Cinemalicious, a unique and vibrant band deeply passionate about the magic of cinema. Founded by a group of film enthusiasts, Cinemalicious is not your typical musical experience – it's an ode to the silver screen. Our melodies are inspired by iconic movie soundtracks, and our lyrics weave tales of cinematic adventures. Join us on a musical journey where each note resonates with the emotions and stories that only the magic of cinema can evoke. Whether you're a film buff or just love good music, Cinemalicious invites you to immerse yourself in the captivating fusion of melodies and movie magic. Let the music roll, and let the reels of imagination spin!
